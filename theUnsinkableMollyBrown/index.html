Ways to Donate

  • Donations can be accepted on Colorado gives each year.
  • A great way to donate is to become a member with us!
  • Each year we hold an Annual Dinner open to the public.
  • Join the cause by donating mobile by texting Molly Brown 57253.
  • Make sure to stay up to date with our calendar and follow us on Facebook to learn more ways to help!

Volunteer

We are always looking for volunteers! Right now, we are in the middle of one of the largest restoration projects and we are in search for the great people that make all of this possible. Sign up now to begin the two-day course that will teach you about the history of the house, artifacts, and Molly Brown.
