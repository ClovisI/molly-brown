Margaret "Molly" Tobin Brown was a both a legend and activist on the Titanic and in the Denver area. Margaret married J.J. Brown in 1886, where they lived in Leadville. After, their courtship the Browns moved to Denver and Margaret began her work in the activist world. Denver Woman's Press Club: Writers Brunch

On October 16th, the Molly Brown House will be coming together, with the Denver Woman's Press Club, for a Writer's Brunch! For many years, DWPC has been supporting young female writers ot put their work out to the public. This event is very important to us and to the legendary Margaret Molly Brown, as she was a founding member of the Denver Woman's Press Club. We will provide food, opportunity, and history.
